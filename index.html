About Us

Our Story & Beliefs

Faith Revival is a non-denomincational church in Hiddenite, NC, with a Pentecostal influence. Our mission is simple: to be the hands and feet of Jesus by living in step with the Holy Spirit. We believe in the freedom to worship, the unity of the body of Christ, and that God's Spirit is alive and working miracle today. Though we are not affiliated with any denomincation or UPC group, we are passionate about creating a place where people can encounter Jesus, grow in faith , and experience the power of His presence.
