Youth Ministry

Raising the Next Generation

Our youth ministry is a vibrant and welcoming community for students to connect with God and with each other. We focus on building a strong foundation of faith through engaging teachings, fun events, and meaningful relationships. We have two classes for ages 2-5 and ages 6+ with four teachers. We host a youth takeover service every odd month and a youth fun day event every even month! Get into contact with the pastors or youth ministry team for more information!

Meeting Times: Every Wednesday at 7:00 PM.
